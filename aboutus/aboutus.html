Abhyan Online Learning Platform

At Abhyan, we bridge the gap between technology and pedagogy to provide an effective learning solution to all who intend to learn & upskill. We offer skill development courses in Indian regional languages to students, graduates and working professionals to be future ready.

Our Vision

To deliver an immersive and interactive online learning experience to our learners in their native language.

To provide a platform for learners and instructors alike to collaborate on on-demand job skills needed today and in future to grow and succeed!

Our Goal is to empower the rural youth of India to be competitive in this Digital era.

  • Leverage the internet to make high-quality technical skills.
  • Bring lessons to life with immersive animations & expert instructors.
  • Cater to a rising regional language user base.

Our LOGO Concept

The logo mimics a three-dimensional object, an inverted book standing on its hardcovers. Lets delve into the reasoning and imagery of the Inverted Book.

The symbol primarily represents the letter 'A' as it is the first word in the company name. It harmonises with the concept of the inverted book as that also follows the outline of the letter. The balance of the image is maintained in the perspective, straight lines blending into curves and the sharp points that give multiple directions a person can set off on to achieve his goals.
